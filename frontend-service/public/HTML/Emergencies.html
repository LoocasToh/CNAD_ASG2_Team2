How the Emergency Button Works

The Emergency Call button is designed for quick access during urgent situations. When pressed, the system asks for confirmation to prevent accidental calls.

  • User presses the Emergency Call button
  • A confirmation prompt appears
  • Once confirmed, the phone immediately calls emergency services
